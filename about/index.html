Shipping Info
Customers are welcome to pick up orders in the store; notification will be sent when the order is complete, and orders can be picked up during business hours any time thereafter.
Completed orders are shipped within one business day once they arrive at the store.
Our standard shipping option is USPS Media Mail which costs $5.00 for the first book plus $1.00 for each additional book. For orders over 5 pounds, additional shipping charges may apply. Media Mail delivery typically takes 5-10 business days. Tracking information is available on request.
Our expedited shipping option is Fed Ex Ground ($6.50, plus $1.00 per additional item). For orders over 5 pounds, additional shipping charges may apply. Ground delivery typically takes 2-5 days. Tracking information available on request.
Gift Certificates are shipped USPS First Class. Gift Certificate shipping is free. First Class typically arrives in 2-3 business days.
For international orders, alternative shipping options, or other questions, please contact us: info@longfellowbooks.com
